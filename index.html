Using Your New Marvin AI Tutor

Once created, you'll find "Marvin" listed on the left sidebar of your Gemini page under "Gems." Just click on his name to start chatting!

  • "Need help with homework?" Just tell Marvin what you're working on, and refer to a specific session! For example, "I need help with my homework on "Session 4: Quadratic Functions and Complex Numbers.""
  • "Assess your understanding:" Ask Marvin for a "quiz" or "self-assessment" on any topic to see how you're doing. You can then request a "tutor report" to get a summary of your work that you can send to your tutor.
  • "Dive deep into a topic:" Ask Marvin to do a "deep dive" or "go into more detail" on any specific curriculum session to get a detailed explanation, more examples, and additional practice problems.
  • "Want to see the curriculum outline?" Ask Marvin to "show me the session outline."
  • "Feeling stuck?" Ask Marvin for a "math joke" for a quick break!
  • "Want to practice a specific lesson?" Ask Marvin to "give me exercises for Session 7" or "review "Session 3: Absolute Value Functions and Equations.""

Marvin also has a built-in survey! If you ever want to give feedback on how he's doing, just type "give feedback" or "take a survey about you" in your chat with him. He'll give you a link to a short survey that helps us make him even better.
